Frequently Asked Questions

Does my card work without internet? ▼

Yes. Once someone opens your card for the first time, it saves to their device automatically. Next time they open it — no internet needed. Your phone number, email, and WhatsApp button all work offline too.

Do they need to install an app? ▼

No app needed at all. Your card is a web link — they tap it, it opens in their browser. They can optionally save it to their home screen for quick access, but nothing to download from the App Store.

How do I share my card? ▼

Your card comes with a unique link and QR code. Share the link via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or Telegram. Show the QR code for them to scan. For Business and Enterprise plans, you also get your own custom web address like makersco.my/yourname.

Can I update my card later? ▼

Yes. Any update you make to your card is instantly live — everyone who opens your link sees the latest version. No need to reprint or reshare. This alone saves you money every time you change your job title, number, or company.

What's the difference between Offline and Online features? ▼

Offline features (like the card itself, QR code, vCard save, and Apple Wallet) work without any internet connection after the first load. Online features (like the contact form, lead notifications, and team dashboard) require internet to send or receive data.

Is this a once-off payment or subscription? ▼

Monthly or annual subscription. Annual plans save 27–30% compared to monthly. Cancel anytime — no lock-in contracts. Your card stays live for the duration of your subscription.

Do you offer setup help? ▼

Yes. All plans come with a setup call via WhatsApp. Enterprise plans include on-site or Zoom training for your team. We handle everything — you just send us your details.
